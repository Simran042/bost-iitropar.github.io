AUTOMOTIVE CLUB

About

Under this Club, you will learn about different technologies related to the automobile sector. The demands from this sector are ever-increasing and so is the need for us to stay updated with the advances. The club will be organising different challenges and competitions so as to enhance the technical skills and take the derived knowledge from the club into action. Moreover, national and international level competitions will be our prime targets. Innovative, fresh,and new challenges will be introduced during the academic session so as to give a small relief from the academics. Apart from these, we will introduce the new challenges in our annual technical event ADVITIYA.
