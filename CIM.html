CIM CLUB

About

Imagination is the beginning of creation. You imagine what you desire. you will what you imagine and at last you create what you will. Welcome to CIM(Computer Integrated Manufacturing) Club of IIT Ropar.A club where imagination comes to reality.This is a student organization club that aims to harness the power of computing for simulations and designing.Over a series of club sessions you will learn software like SolidWorks, AutoCAD, ANSYS which help you make your imagination and creativity live.It mainly involves data integrity and process control, the two main strengths of a production unit.Things which you are going to learn in this club is very essential for mechanical and civil engineers will also help in your academics as well as research projects.There are no prerequisites to join this club.Learning these skills has helped the members in past in their projects, club projects, internships and it adds immensely valuable skill to your resume.
