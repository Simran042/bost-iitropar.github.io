Student Outreach Session-2

Introduction

Board Of Science and Technology, IIT Ropar in collaboration with Pehchaan-Ek Safar conducted first session of School Outreach Programme at Shiwalik Public School, Ropar on 18 May 2019.The session was held by the BOST members of 2k19.

About the Session

The topic of the session was interfacing sensors with ArduinoStudents learnt about basic programming of Arduino. They learnt how to use Arduino IDE, upload sketch on the board and to use sensors with Arduino for the desired outputs. They designed pattern using blinking LEDs with help of Arduino.
