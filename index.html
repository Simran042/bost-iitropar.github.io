  • Robotics Club

    The Robotics Club at its core is driven by fascination with being able to build things that emulate real life, or finding solutions to real-world problems through devices or bots that are a product of our collective intelligence. Developing new skills which would help us channel this fascination into tangible implementation is our primary goal. We are a community of technology enthusiasts, which envisions to build things too fantastic to be true, as if straight out of a book or a movie.





  • Aeromodelling Club

    The desire to fly is an idea handed down to us by our ancestors who...looked enviously on the birds soaring freely through space...on the infinite highway of the air. We, at Aeromodelling Club, IIT Ropar are group of aeromodelling and aviation enthusiasts with a passion for flying and an innate desire to push the bounds of airspace like they have never been before.





  • Coding Club

    • The Coding Club, being the largest club in college,is instrumental in setting up the right atmosphere for competitive coding in the college. Alongwith enabling people to develop the unparalleled skills for coding their way through external competitions, the club often holds contests of its own to boast the coding skills of the college. A community which allows for only logical reasoning and which does not believe in settling on algorithms, we are always on the quest for the better, after all there is no ‘best’ in a competition.




  • Software Community

    Software innovation, like almost every other kind of innovation, requires the ability to collaborate and share ideas with other people, and to sit down and talk with other members and get their feedback and understand their view point. Our vision is to unleash the full potential of the amazing software engineers in IIT Ropar by providing whatever resources required.




  • CIM Club

    CIM club harnesses the power of computing for simulations and designing.Computer-integrated manufacturing (CIM) is the manufacturing approach of using computers to control the entire production process.This club bolsters 2D and 3D designing of anything and everything you can imagine.This club also deals with computer simulations of various systems CIM is an example of the implementation of information and communication technologies (ICTs) in manufacturing.




  • Zentih Club

    Zenith club is for all those who look up at the sky and wonder. We conduct observation sessions to look into the night sky through our Telescope, conduct quizzes on current and basic astronomy topics. We organize documentary screening sessions to simplify the learning experience and encourage discussions so as to form a community and enhance our knowledge. We also do amateur Astro-photography. We invite distinguished professors for guest lectures who have contributed their lives in this field. Everyone is welcome to all these events. No prior knowledge in astronomy is required, just interest and passion for the heavens above is all we need!




  • Finance Community

    Its not a hobby, its not a passion, its a basic need of life that each and every one of us needs whether a middle-class businessman or a CEO of MNC. Fincom is a non core club but if someone is enthusiastic about it and has the caliber of becoming a core in itself the club opens the wide door of analytics and consultancy for one and apart from this the club pulls out the hidden problem solver that hides inside all of us by making ones mind strome through various case problems throughout the year, these problem solving skills are not just needed for this field but these are the skills that every engineer needs throughout his life.




  • Monochrome Club

    The club Monochrome is driven by the motivation of exploring without any constraint, where our imagination are your words and your skills your pen, and when you blend them together, you write beautiful poetry that perfectly reflects your ideology. Bringing out the best out of the provided is also one of our motives To provide basic knowledge of Digital design to the upcoming designers of IIT Ropar. Also Provide them hands on experience in photoshop , illustrator and premiere pro through design labs and help them gain experience in digital designing/editing via enabling them to give their contribution in organising various events at IIT Ropar throughout the year. So if you think designing is for you, then MONOChrome should definitely be on your list.




  • Automotive Club

    This quote by Sir Ford was the motivation for the people who started the Automotive Club of IIT Ropar and these continuous efforts encourages the students to put their knowledge and imagination together for the astonishing outputs. Under the umbrella of the club, one will be participating in different competitions, learning about technologies and mechanics related to the automobile. Apart from this, the sessions on Computer Integrated Manufacturing will be arranged in collaboration with CIM Club, IIT Ropar.


Innovative Programs and Technostructure

Sci-Fi

Working on the project not only requires ones skills but also demands a well-equipped working area. BOST provides the sci-fi room where one can work on the projects. It consists of several labs shared by the various clubs of BOST. The room is open 24*7 for the technical enthusiasts and provides all the necessary equipments for the projects.

BOST Summer Camp

Summer holidays provide students the opportunity to learn new things while having fun at the same time. The Board Of Science And Technology comes up with the opportunity for enthusiastic students to show case their talents by providing a platform through the Summer Camp Program. If you have any technical idea, which may lead to useful product or demonstration, BOST with its various clubs can help you technically as well as with resources. Under this program students can choose projects of their interests and work on their ideas, spending their summer breaks in a productive manner.

Advitiya

The annual technical festival of IIT ropar “Adivtiya ”, marks a 3 day journey consisting of various competitions spread across Robotics, Management, Coding, Designing, Gaming, Astronomy, Aero-modelling and Quizzing. Apart from the competitions, talks and workshops are few of the interesting events of the fest. The upcoming Advitiya is going to be held in February 2020.

Quintessence

IITs are not only the technological hub but are also known for amalgamating education with fun-filled extra-curricular activities. Every year BOST organizes IIT Ropar’s intra-college technical fest ‘Quintessence’. Continuing with that tradition, we are pleased to announce that Quintessence’19 will be organized in the month of November. As a part of this, clubs under BOST will be organizing several enlightening and interesting talks by eminent speakers and a multitude of competitions under Coding, Designing, Gaming, Astronomy, Aero-modelling, Robotics and Quizzing. Students of the institute make a great show of their talents in various fields by participating in these events.
